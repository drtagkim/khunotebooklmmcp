### Strategic Superiority
The proposed index is not just a measurement tool but a strategic asset. Its superiority lies in its ability to **predict** and **prescribe**.

1.  **Predictive Power**: By measuring R&D inputs (Creativity/Innovation) and early-stage social signals, the index can predict which content types are likely to explode in specific regions *before* sales data confirms it.
2.  **Diagnostic Capability**: Unlike existing indices that say "Sales are down," the new index can diagnose *why*. (e.g., "Creativity score is high, but 'Press' (Distribution) score is low" -> Problem is marketing, not content).
3.  **Policy Precision**: Allows government and investors to target support where the ecosystem is weak (e.g., investing in translation AI if the 'Language Barrier' metric is high).

### Case Study Simulation
*   *Scenario*: K-Webtoon expansion in France.
*   *Old Index*: Shows "Low Awareness" (Lagging).
*   *New Index*: Shows "High Novelty Score" + "Rising Niche Search Volume" -> **Signal to Invest**.
